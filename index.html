Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for working with you?

We begin with a complimentary 30-minute Discovery Session where we explore your goals and determine if we're a good fit. If we decide to move forward, you'll choose your coaching package (3 or 6 months), sign your commitment contract, and we'll schedule your biweekly sessions. Each 60-90 minute session focuses on deep questioning, body awareness exercises, and practical tools for transformation. You'll receive personalized homework assignments between sessions to accelerate your growth.

How do you select clients to work with?

I'm highly selective and work with people who are genuinely ready for deep transformation. During our Discovery Session, I assess your commitment level, openness to change, and alignment with my coaching approach. I only accept clients who are prepared to do the inner work required for lasting change. This ensures the best outcomes for everyone involved.

What can I expect in our sessions?

Our sessions are not traditional therapy where you talk about your week. Instead, we dive deep into your patterns, beliefs, and goals through targeted questioning. I'll guide you through body awareness exercises, breathing techniques, and help you identify where emotions live in your body. You'll leave each session with specific practices and assignments designed for your unique journey.

Do you offer refunds?

No, I don't offer refunds because you can't refund my time, energy, and the deep work we'll do together. Transformation requires commitment and consistency. The no-refund policy ensures you're fully invested in your growth process. This commitment to showing up for yourself is part of what creates lasting change.

What if I miss a scheduled session?

If you give advance notice and need to reschedule, we can work together to find a new time. However, if you simply miss a scheduled session without notice, that session is forfeited. This policy supports your commitment to yourself and respects the time we've both set aside for your transformation.

How is text support different from sessions?

Text support allows you to share insights, ask questions, and receive guidance between sessions. I respond within 24-48 hours with thoughtful feedback, mini-exercises, or clarifications. This keeps your momentum going and provides support when you're processing between our biweekly meetings.

How do I know if this work is right for me?

This work is ideal if you're tired of surface-level changes and ready for deep transformation. You should be open to examining your patterns, willing to do homework assignments, and committed to showing up consistently. If you're looking for quick fixes or aren't ready to look deeply at yourself, this probably isn't the right fit.
